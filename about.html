The Problem

According to the World Health Organization, 2.2 billion people worldwide do not have safe drinking water. In the Philippines, 1 in 10 families still struggles to access clean water, and 1 in 5 lacks basic sanitation facilities.

Our Advocacy

Our mission supports SDG 6: ensuring access to clean water and sanitation for all. We believe water is a right, not a privilege.
